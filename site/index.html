FAQ

Questions? Tackled.

I'm not a producer — what exactly does TuneTackle do?

When you record your voice at home, it sounds thin and amateur compared to songs on Spotify. That difference is called "mixing" — a technical process engineers charge hundreds of dollars for. TuneTackle is software that does that process for you, automatically, so your recordings sound professionally produced.

What's a DAW, and will TuneTackle work with mine?

A DAW (Digital Audio Workstation) is the program you record music in — GarageBand, Ableton Live, FL Studio, Logic Pro, Pro Tools, and so on. TuneTackle installs as a standard plugin (VST3 and AU formats), which means it works inside every major DAW on Mac and Windows.

Do I need any mixing experience?

None. Pick a preset that matches your genre, then adjust one knob — Polish — to taste. If you're more advanced, every module (Tune, Clean, EQ, Compress, Air, Space) opens up for detailed control. It grows with you.

Will it work with my microphone and home setup?

Yes — TuneTackle was designed for real-world home recordings. Its cleanup stage is built to handle untreated rooms, budget microphones, and laptop setups. The worse your room sounds, the more dramatic the difference.
